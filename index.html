Welcome to Animal Charity Organization Punjab

Dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating animals in need across Punjab, India. Your support can save lives!

Featured Programs

Street Animal Rescue

We provide emergency care and rehabilitation for street animals that have been abandoned, injured, or mistreated.

Animal Shelter & Rehabilitation

Our shelter is a safe haven for animals that need medical care and rehabilitation, giving them a second chance at life.

Adoption Program

Find your perfect companion through our adoption program, which connects rescued animals with loving families.

Our Mission

We believe every animal deserves a life free from suffering. Our mission is to provide care, shelter, and medical attention to animals in distress while educating the public about animal rights and welfare.

Our Programs

Community Education

We educate communities about animal care, responsible pet ownership, and the importance of spaying and neutering to control the population of street animals.

Volunteer Program

Join our team of passionate volunteers who work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need.

Spay/Neuter Campaign

We offer spay and neuter services to control the population of street animals and reduce suffering caused by overpopulation.

Donate Now

Your generous donation helps us continue our mission to save animals in need. Every contribution makes a difference in the lives of vulnerable animals. By donating, you directly support our rescue missions, medical care, shelter services, and adoption programs. Together, we can make a lasting impact on the lives of countless animals.

Get in Touch

If you have any questions or would like to get involved, we'd love to hear from you! Reach out via email or phone, or visit us at our shelter. Whether you're interested in volunteering, adopting a pet, or learning more about our programs, we're here to connect and collaborate with compassionate individuals like you.
